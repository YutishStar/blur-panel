          cracked hackerhouse exists to forge and connect ambitious builders
        
    
        
    
          
    
            traditionally, ambitious builders like yourself have been constrained by the
            time requirements of school and work.
          
    
          
    
            we run 8 week-long cohorts so you can go full time on your craft. we provide
            housing, a workspace, and the people so you can live and ship with
            equally cracked peers.
          
    
          
    
            you can think of us as “college” meets “residency”.

    what to expect
    

        
    
          
    
            use demo day to prove your craft to potential collaborators,
            employees, and investors — like a16z, 1517 fund, and f.
          
    
          
    
            stay clear minded and accountable with weekly syncs and
            coaching sessions.
          
    
          
    
            connect with someone who will 10x your trajectory through intros and
            fireside chats — with people like sam altman,
            kevin hartz, and danielle strachman.
          
    
          
    
            most of all, expect spontaneous adventures and late night conversations with
            your fellow residents… living the joy of building together is
            what really makes the house. ❤
          
    
        
    

        
        
        
        
        
      
    
    
    

    
    
    
      
    questions?
    

      
    
        
    
          
            are there any perks?
            
          
          
    
            
    yes! lots of them. residents get compute credits, food discounts, founder office hours, and even free therapy.
    
          
    
        
    
        
    
          
            do i need to be a founder?
            
          
          
    
            
    no. about a third of every cohort joins solo, mid-project, or between things. we accept people, not stages.
    
          
    
        
    
        
    
          
            how long is the program?
            
          
          
    
            
    8 weeks per cohort. we run three cohorts per year — summer, fall, and winter.
    
          
    
        
    
        
    
          
            do you help with visas?
            
          
          
    
            
    yes. we sponsor short-term visas for our international residents and run satellite houses in countries with easier entry for those who need them.
    
          
    
        
    
        
    
          
            can i do this online?
            
          
          
    
            
    no. the magic of the house is being in the same room as the rest of the cohort — cooking, debugging, and shipping side by side.
    
          
    
        
    
        
    
          
            can i be in school or have a job?
            
          
          
    
            
    part-time, maybe. full-time, no. the cohort works best when everyone is in deep on the same thing. you don't have to ship a startup — but you have to be all the way in for 8 weeks.
